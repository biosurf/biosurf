Integrating and co-clustering CyTOF and scRNA-seq

Søren H Dam

Last updated on 2026-06-17

This vignette demonstrates integrating a same-sample scRNA-seq and CyTOF experiment (Su et al.). The purpose is to improve their individual annotations through a co-clustered annotation. To assimilate scRNA-seq data to CyTOF, we use our own implementation of Single-cell nearest-neighbor pseudobulking (scennep).

Download data

We got the download instructions from the article’s Data availability section. CyTOF is available on FlowRepository (FR-FCM-Z6ZN) and scRNA-seq data is available on SRA (SRR23497303). Getting the scRNA-seq metadata took some looking around, but direct download links are presented in the code chunks.
